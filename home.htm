My work

Oil panting

Oil painting is a traditional artistic technique that uses pigments mixed with a binder, usually linseed oil, to create vibrant and durable artworks. The slow drying time of oil paint allows for blending and layering, enabling artists to achieve rich textures and intricate details in their compositions.

Sketch

Sketch art involves the use of quick, spontaneous, and freehand drawing techniques to capture the essence of a subject. Typically done with pencils, charcoal, or ink, sketches often serve as initial drafts or expressive renderings, emphasizing simplicity and fluidity in the artistic process.

Water colour

Watercolor painting utilizes pigments suspended in a water-based solution, creating translucent and luminous artworks. Artists often exploit the medium's fluidity, allowing colors to blend organically on paper, resulting in delicate and expressive compositions with a distinctive freshness.
